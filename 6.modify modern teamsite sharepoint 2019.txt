step-by-step guide on how to modify a SharePoint 2019 modern team site's name and logo. 


**Step 1: Access Site Information**
Open your SharePoint site and go to the **Site information** settings menu.

**Step 2: Change the Site Name**
In the **Site name** field, delete the default text (such as "home") and type in your desired name, for example, "harethsoft website".

**Step 3: Update Site Description (Optional)**
If you wish to provide more context for the site, you can modify the **Site description** field. In the demonstration, this was left as the default.

**Step 4: Change the Site Logo (Optional)**
To update the visual branding, you can change the **Site logo** by uploading a new image from your computer.

**Step 5: Save Your Changes**
Click the **Save** button. Once saved, the new site name and the new logo image will immediately appear on your site's front page.

**Step 6: Apply to Other Areas**
You can use this same simple method to change the front page name for any other site or subsite within your SharePoint environment.
