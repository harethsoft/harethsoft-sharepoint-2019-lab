
### **Summary of Steps: Enabling Target Audience in SharePoint 2019**

**1. Library Activation**

* Go to **Site Pages** > **Library Settings**.
* Click on **Audience Targeting Settings**.
* Check the box: **Enable Audience Targeting** and click **OK**.

**2. Assigning Groups to News (The Manual Way)**

* Go to **Site Pages** library.
* Click the **three dots (...)** next to the News article.
* Select **More** > **Properties** (or **Edit Item**).
* Locate the field named **Target Audiences**.
* Type the specific group name (e.g., `Finance_Group` or `IT_Group`).
* Click **Save**.

**3. Homepage Web Part Configuration**

* Go to the **Home Page** and click **Edit**.
* Edit the **News Web Part** (click the pencil icon).
* Switch the **Enable Audience Targeting** toggle to **On**.
* **Publish** the page.
